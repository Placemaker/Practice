Jan Gehl

Making Cities for People

Jan is an Architect, Founding Partner of Gehl, and former Professor and Researcher at The Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts, School of Architecture.

Over the course of his career, he has published several books, including, “Life Between Buildings”, “Cities for People”, “New City Spaces”, “Public Spaces – Public Life”, “New City Life” and most recently “How to Study Public Life”. As part of Gehl, Jan has collaborated on projects for the cities of Copenhagen, London, Melbourne, Sydney, New York and Moscow, among others.

He is an honorary fellow of RIBA, AIA, RAIC, and PIA. See the story of Jan Gehl and Gehl here.
